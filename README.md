# CampaignOS — AI-Powered Campaign Intelligence Operating System

CampaignOS is a full-stack AI-native campaign intelligence platform designed to automate strategic marketing operations using LLM-driven intelligence pipelines, behavioral signal extraction, creator intelligence systems, workflow orchestration, and strategic memory retrieval.

The system transforms a simple campaign brief into:

* audience behavioral intelligence
* strategic campaign narratives
* creator recommendations
* rollout workflows
* operational execution plans

through a modular multi-stage AI pipeline.

# System Pipeline

## Step 1 — Campaign Input

User submits:

* brand
* product
* audience
* platforms
* campaign goals

---

## Step 2 — Intelligence Retrieval

The retrieval orchestrator gathers:

* trend signals
* creator intelligence
* audience conversations
* social patterns
* competitor intelligence

---

## Step 3 — Behavioral Signal Extraction

LLM analyzes retrieval evidence and extracts:

* psychological signals
* emotional motivators
* trust behaviors
* engagement patterns

---

## Step 4 — Strategy Generation

AI strategist generates:

* campaign narrative
* content pillars
* hooks
* rollout strategy
* creator positioning

---

## Step 5 — Creator Matching

Scoring engine evaluates:

* audience alignment
* content style compatibility
* engagement quality

and recommends optimal creators.

---

## Step 6 — Workflow Generation

Operational AI engine creates:

* rollout workflows
* execution phases
* dependencies
* approvals
* risk analysis

---

## Step 7 — Strategic Memory Storage

Campaign intelligence is stored in vector memory for future retrieval and learning.
